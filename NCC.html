Tournament Rules and Regulations

1. General Rules:

  • All games will be played according to FIDE Laws of Chess
  • Players must arrive 15 minutes before their scheduled games
  • Mobile phones and electronic devices must be switched off during play
  • Players found using any assistance will be immediately disqualified

2. Time Control:

  • Different time controls will apply based on age categories
  • No draws will be agreed in less than 30 moves
  • Arbiters' decisions are final and binding

3. Conduct:

  • Players must maintain proper chess etiquette at all times
  • Any form of unsporting behavior will result in penalties
  • Parents and spectators must remain quiet during games

4. Registration:

  • Registration fees are non-refundable once paid
  • Players must compete in their correct age category
  • Proof of age may be requested

5. Liability:

  • The organizing committee is not responsible for any personal belongings
  • Players participate at their own risk
  • Tournament organizers reserve the right to modify rules if necessary

6. Prizes and Awards:

  • Prizes will be distributed according to the published prize structure
  • Players must be present during the closing ceremony to collect prizes
  • Decisions regarding prize distribution are final

By accepting these terms, you acknowledge that you have read and understood all the rules and regulations.

Please ensure you agree to all terms before proceeding to registration.
